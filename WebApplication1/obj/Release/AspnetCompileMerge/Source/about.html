Welcome to Easy Healthcare Hospital

Best Medical & Health care Needs to Our Patients

Easy Healthcare Hospital is an American multinational hospital chain headquartered in Los Angelas, Long Beach, Indiana, and NY. It was founded in 1983 as the first corporate healthcare provider in Indiana. Several of Easy Healthcare's hospitals have been among the first in India to receive international healthcare accreditation by the America-based Joint Commission International (JCI), as well as 13 NABH National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers hospitals.

In addition to providing hospitals, US's has developed services in telemedicine, after starting a pilot project in 2000 at Aragonda. US's signed an MoU with Health Education England in April 2017 to provide a large number of doctors to fill vacancies in the English National Health Service.

  • Easy Healthcare Pharmacy was started in 1987. It is with over 4000+ outlets in over 21 states.
  • Hyderabad-based pharmacy chain Hetero Med Solutions Ltd (HMSL) was acquired by Easy Healthcare Pharmacy (Easy Healthcare Hospitals Enterprise Ltd) in 2014. HMSL had around 320 stores across many parts in the states.
